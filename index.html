Who We Are

An Institute Built for Practitioners, by Industry.

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute specialising in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments. We are focused on practical capability, not generic theory.

Our programmes are built and continuously reviewed in partnership with a UK university, ensuring academic credibility and access to leading research. Every cohort works on live industry problems aligned to frameworks such as DORA, NIS2, and ISO/IEC 27001, so learning translates directly into job performance.

UK University Partnership
Academic credibility grounded in leading research and co-designed curriculum.
Live Industry Projects
Every cohort works on real problems with real organisations — no simulations.
Immediate Deployment
Graduates enter roles ready to operate, not requiring six months of onboarding.

Our Model

Train. Innovate. Deploy.

Three interconnected pillars that take a practitioner from first principles to frontline deployment.

Training & Qualifications

IDR Academy

Structured bootcamps and professional programmes for students, career changers, and working professionals seeking to build or deepen competence in digital and cyber risk. Programmes are mapped to DORA, NIS2, ISO/IEC 27001, and NIST-aligned practice.

Implementation & Compliance

Advisory Services

IDR's advisory practice helps organisations in regulated sectors design, implement, and evidence compliance with leading risk frameworks including NIST, ISO/IEC 27001, NIS2, and DORA. Engagements range from focused gap reviews to embedded delivery support.
